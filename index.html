Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign advised by Ravishankar Iyer and Zbigniew Kalbarczyk in the DEPEND group at the Coordinated Science Laboratory. My current work focuses on dependability assessment and design of fault-tolerant large-scale High-Performance Computing and Autonomous Systems.

I am currently working on “Holistic, Measurement-Driven Resilience”, HMDSA, “Computer System Failure Data Repository to Enable Data-Driven Dependability Research”, and “Dependability of Autonomous Vehicles” projects. I collaborate with National Center for Supercomputing Applications, Sandia National Lab, Los Almos National Lab, National Energy Research Scientific Computing Center, Cray, Bell Labs, Microsoft Research , and Nvidia Corporation. I have previously interned at Microsoft Research (MSR) Redmond in 2015 and 2016, and Nvidia Research Labs in 2018.
