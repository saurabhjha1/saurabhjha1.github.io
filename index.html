Biography

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at UIUC, where I work with Prof. Ravishankar Iyer in the DEPEND group. My research focuses on design of techniques for automatic identification of safety-critical bugs, and smart-monitoring of systems and applications. My work is supported through NSF, DoE, Sandia National Laboratories, IBM, and NVIDIA.
