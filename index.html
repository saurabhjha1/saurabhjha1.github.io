Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). advised by Ravishankar Iyer and Zbigniew Kalbarczyk in the DEPEND research group at the Coordinated Science Laboratory. My current work focuses on assessment and design of resillience- and performance-aware computing systems (e.g., datacenters and autonomous systems).

I have ongoing collaborations with National Center for Supercomputing Applications, Sandia National Lab, National Energy Research Scientific Computing Center, Los Almos National Lab, Cray, Bell Labs, Microsoft Research, Nvidia Corporation, Samsung Research and IBM. In the past, I have collaborated with Institute of High-Performance Computing at A* STAR and NeST.
