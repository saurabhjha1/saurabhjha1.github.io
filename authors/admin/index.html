Saurabh Jha

PhD Student

University of Illinois at Urbana - Champaign (UIUC)

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at UIUC, where I work with Prof. Ravishankar Iyer in the DEPEND group. My research interests include design and assessment of resilience- and performance-aware computer systems with a focus on datacenters and autonomous systems.
